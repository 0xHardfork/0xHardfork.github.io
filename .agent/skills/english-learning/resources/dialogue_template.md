## Notes

- All dialogues should sound natural and conversational
- Avoid overly formal or literary language unless appropriate for the context
- Include common expressions and idioms that native speakers actually use
- Grammar explanations should be clear and practical
- Vocabulary should focus on useful, frequently-used words
