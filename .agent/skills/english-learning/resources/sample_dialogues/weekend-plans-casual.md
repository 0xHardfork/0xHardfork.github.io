# Weekend Plans (Example)

**Type**: Casual Communication  
**Difficulty**: 初级 (Elementary)  
**Date**: 2026-01-31  
**Category**: Daily Life

---

## 中文版

**小明**: 嘿，这周末你有什么计划吗？

**小红**: 我还没想好。你呢？

**小明**: 我想去看电影。你想一起去吗？

**小红**: 好啊！看什么电影？

**小明**: 有一部新的动作片。你喜欢动作片吗？

**小红**: 喜欢！什么时候去？

**小明**: 周六下午怎么样？

**小红**: 完美！几点见面？

**小明**: 两点在电影院门口见，好吗？

**小红**: 好的！到时候见！

## English Version

**Xiao Ming**: Hey, do you have any plans this weekend?

**Xiao Hong**: I haven't decided yet. How about you?

**Xiao Ming**: I want to go see a movie. Do you want to come with me?

**Xiao Hong**: Sure! What movie?

**Xiao Ming**: There's a new action film. Do you like action movies?

**Xiao Hong**: I do! When should we go?

**Xiao Ming**: How about Saturday afternoon?

**Xiao Hong**: Perfect! What time should we meet?

**Xiao Ming**: Let's meet at 2 PM in front of the cinema, okay?

**Xiao Hong**: Sounds good! See you then!

### Grammar Explanations

#### 1. Present Perfect - "I haven't decided"

- **Example from dialogue**: "I haven't decided yet."
- **Structure**: have/has + not + past participle
- **Usage**: Describes an action that hasn't happened up to the present moment
- **中文说明**: 现在完成时否定形式，表示到现在为止还没有做某事

#### 2. "How about" - Suggestions

- **Example from dialogue**: "How about you?" / "How about Saturday afternoon?"
- **Structure**: How about + noun/gerund
- **Usage**: Used to make suggestions or ask about alternatives
- **中文说明**: 用于提出建议或询问选择，相当于"...怎么样？"

#### 3. "Let's" - Suggestions for joint action

- **Example from dialogue**: "Let's meet at 2 PM"
- **Structure**: Let's + base verb
- **Usage**: Suggests doing something together
- **中文说明**: 提议一起做某事，"让我们..."

### Vocabulary

| English | Part of Speech | Chinese | Example from Dialogue |
|---------|---------------|---------|----------------------|
| plans | noun | 计划 | "do you have any plans" |
| decide | verb | 决定 | "I haven't decided yet" |
| action film | noun phrase | 动作片 | "a new action film" |
| perfect | adjective | 完美的 | "Perfect!" |
| meet | verb | 见面 | "What time should we meet?" |
| cinema | noun | 电影院 | "in front of the cinema" |

**Key Phrases:**

- **"How about you?"** - 你呢？ - Asking the same question back to someone
- **"Do you want to come with me?"** - 你想和我一起去吗？ - Inviting someone
- **"Sounds good!"** - 好的！听起来不错！ - Agreeing to a suggestion
- **"See you then!"** - 到时候见！ - Saying goodbye with plans to meet later

## 日本語版

**小明**: ねえ、今週末は何か予定ある？

**小紅**: まだ決めてないんだ。あなたは？

**小明**: 映画を見に行きたいんだ。一緒に行かない？

**小紅**: いいね！何の映画？

**小明**: 新しいアクション映画があるよ。アクション映画好き？

**小紅**: 好き！いつ行く？

**小明**: 土曜日の午後はどう？

**小紅**: 完璧！何時に会う？

**小明**: 2時に映画館の前で会おう、いい？

**小紅**: いいよ！じゃあ、そのときに！

### 文法説明 (Grammar Explanations)

#### 1. 「〜ない？」- Casual invitation/suggestion

- **例文**: "一緒に行かない？"
- **文法**: Negative form used as invitation (inverted meaning)
- **使い方**: Casual way to invite someone, literally "won't you go?" but means "want to go?"
- **中文说明**: 用否定形式表示邀请，是一种随意的邀请方式

#### 2. 「〜はどう？」- Making suggestions

- **例文**: "土曜日の午後はどう？"
- **文法**: [noun] + はどう？
- **使い方**: Asking for opinion or making a suggestion
- **中文说明**: 征求意见或提出建议，"...怎么样？"

#### 3. 「〜よう」- Let's (volitional form)

- **例文**: "会おう"
- **文法**: Verb volitional form
- **使い方**: Suggesting to do something together
- **中文说明**: 意志形，表示"让我们..."，提议一起做某事

### 語彙 (Vocabulary)

| 日本語 | 品詞 | 中文 | 例文 |
|--------|------|------|------|
| 予定(よてい) | 名詞 | 计划 | "何か予定ある？" |
| 決める(きめる) | 動詞 | 决定 | "まだ決めてない" |
| 映画(えいが) | 名詞 | 电影 | "映画を見に行く" |
| 一緒(いっしょ) | 名詞 | 一起 | "一緒に行かない？" |
| 午後(ごご) | 名詞 | 下午 | "土曜日の午後" |
| 映画館(えいがかん) | 名詞 | 电影院 | "映画館の前で" |

**重要フレーズ:**

- **「いいね！」** - 好啊！不错！ - Expressing agreement or approval
- **「〜はどう？」** - ...怎么样？ - Making suggestions
- **「じゃあ、そのときに！」** - 那到时候见！ - Saying goodbye with plans to meet
- **「〜んだ」** - Explanatory casual ending
