### Grammar Explanations

1. **Structure**: Present continuous with 'been doing' (Present Perfect Continuous)
   - **Example from dialogue**: "I've been digging into their docs."
   - **Explanation**: Used to express an action that started in the past and continues to the present, emphasizing the ongoing nature of the activity. 用于表达从过去开始一直持续到现在的一个动作，强调动作的持续性。

2. **Structure**: Idiomatic phrasal verbs
   - **Example from dialogue**: "Let's knock it out fast."
   - **Explanation**: "Knock out" informally means to complete a task quickly and effectively. 非正式表达，意为快速高效地完成一项任务。

### 文法説明 (Grammar Explanations)

1. **文法**: ～ところかな / ～かな
   - **例**: 定期課金のところかな。
   - **説明**: 自分の意見や推測を柔らかく伝える言い方。"Maybe it's the..." or "I guess it's the..." に相当します。一种用来柔和表达自己意见或推测的说法。

2. **文法**: ～なきゃいけない (～なければいけないの口語表現)
   - **例**: 根本的に作り直さなきゃいけないんだ。
   - **説明**: 「～しなければならない」のカジュアルな会話表現。"Have to" or "Must" done. “必须做……”的口语表达。
