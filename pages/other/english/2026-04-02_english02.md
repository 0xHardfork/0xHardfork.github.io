### Grammar Explanations

1. **Structure**: Phrasal verb "pull up"
   - **Example from dialogue**: Sure, pull it up.
   - **Explanation**: To display something on a computer screen. (在电脑屏幕上显示、打开某物。)

2. **Structure**: Phrase "spike on"
   - **Example from dialogue**: I'll spike on the upgrade path
   - **Explanation**: A "spike" in Agile development meaning to do a quick, time-boxed investigation or prototype to figure something out. (在敏捷开发中是指进行快速的限时调研或原型设计，以弄清某个问题。)

3. **Structure**: "Good catch"
   - **Example from dialogue**: Good catch.
   - **Explanation**: An informal way to praise someone for noticing an error, bug, or problem. (常用于非正式场合，称赞别人发现了一个错误、漏洞或问题。)

### 文法説明 (Grammar Explanations)

1. **文法**: 〜が怒っている (figurative: the system is complaining)
   - **例**: スキャナーが怒っているよ
   - **説明**: 拟人化表达。扫描器等系统因为有错误而频繁报警或拒绝执行时，常被形容为系统“生气了”。

2. **文法**: 場当たり的な (ad hoc/quick hack)
   - **例**: 場当たり的な修正をしたみたいだ
   - **説明**: 意思是为了应付眼前情况，没有经过深思熟虑就做出的临时对策。
