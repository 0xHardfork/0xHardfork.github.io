### Grammar Explanations

1. **Structure**: Phrasal verb "wrap up"
   - **Example from dialogue**: Hoping to wrap it up by EOD.
   - **Explanation**: To finish or complete something successfully. EOD means "End Of Day". (圆满完成结束日常工作。EOD 是 End Of Day 意思指下班前。)

2. **Structure**: Phrasal verb "look into"
   - **Example from dialogue**: I started looking into the slow query issue.
   - **Explanation**: To investigate or examine a problem. (调查或检查某个问题。)

3. **Structure**: "On your end"
   - **Example from dialogue**: How are things on your end?
   - **Explanation**: A common way to ask someone about the situation or progress of the work they are responsible for. (用来询问对方负责的工作进展的常用表达，意为“你那边”。)

### 文法説明 (Grammar Explanations)

1. **文法**: サクッと (quickly, easily)
   - **例**: サクッとシンクしようか
   - **説明**: 意思是指快速、轻松地进行某事。职场上经常用来提议做个简短的会议或讨论。

2. **文法**: 叩く (to run/execute a command)
   - **例**: EXPLAINコマンドは叩いてみた？
   - **説明**: 在IT语境下，“敲（键盘）”引申为执行某个命令或敲某个 API。
