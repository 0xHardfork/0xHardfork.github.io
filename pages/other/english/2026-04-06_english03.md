### Grammar Explanations

1. **Structure**: Phrase "spinning its wheels"
   - **Example from dialogue**: It's just Outlook spinning its wheels.
   - **Explanation**: To invest effort or try to do something with no result or progress. Often used for programs that are loading indefinitely. (做无用功或者是原地不动、没有进展。常用来形容软件程序陷入死循环或一直在加载中。)

2. **Structure**: Phrasal verb "act up"
   - **Example from dialogue**: Reach out if it starts acting up again!
   - **Explanation**: To not work properly or to behave badly. (出故障，无法正常运转，或是捣乱/调皮。)

3. **Structure**: Phrase "bail out"
   - **Example from dialogue**: Thanks for bailing me out.
   - **Explanation**: To rescue someone from a difficult situation. (把人从困境或者烂摊子中解救出来。)

### 文法説明 (Grammar Explanations)

1. **文法**: ぐるぐる回る/空回りする (spinning its wheels)
   - **例**: Outlookだけがずっとぐるぐる回ってる
   - **説明**: 形容应用加载界面上的加载圈“转个不停”，或者引申为只转圈不工作、没有进展。

2. **文法**: 調子がおかしくなる (act up)
   - **例**: また調子がおかしくなったら
   - **説明**: 意思是指身体或机器的状态“变得奇怪”或“运转不良”，相当于“出故障”“闹脾气”。
